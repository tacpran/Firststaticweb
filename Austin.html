Population

Austin has a diverse population with a mix of cultures and ethnicities. As of the latest census, the population is around 2,228,000 people an increase of 2.39% since 2022.

Incorporation

Region

Austin is located in Central Texas and is characterized by the beautiful Hill Country landscape. The Colorado River runs through the city, adding to its scenic beauty.

Classification

Austin is considered an urban city.

Related Information

Austin is the state capital of Texas, an inland city bordering the Hill Country region. Home to the University of Texas flagship campus, Austin is known for its eclectic live-music scene centered around country, blues and rock. Its many parks and lakes are popular for hiking, biking, swimming and boating. South of the city, Formula One's Circuit of the Americas raceway has hosted the United States Grand Prix.
