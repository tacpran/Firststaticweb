Population

El Paso has a rich cultural heritage, with a population that includes a mix of Hispanic, Mexican, and American influences. The population is around 723,511 people.

Incorporation

El Paso was incorporated in 1850

Region

El Paso is situated in the western part of Texas, with the Rio Grande forming the border between the United States and Mexico. The Franklin Mountains provide a stunning backdrop to the city.

Classification

El Paso, TX is considered an urban area

Average income compared to the rest of the state

El Paso Household average income: $57,298
State Average Household Income: $72,284

Related Information

Centuries-old Spanish missions, art museums, theaters, operas, symphony orchestras, outstanding Mexican and international cuisine, mariachi bands, rodeos, and country and western dancing all play a part in El Paso's cultural mosaic.
