Population

San Antonio is a diverse city with a population of over 1.5 million people. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, influenced by Mexican, Spanish, and Native American traditions.

Region

San Antonio is located in south-central Texas and is characterized by its scenic River Walk along the San Antonio River. The city is known for historical sites such as the Alamo and the beautiful San Antonio Missions.
